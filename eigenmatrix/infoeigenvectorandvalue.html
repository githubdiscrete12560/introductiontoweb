The Determinant / ‡∏î‡∏µ‡πÄ‡∏ó‡∏≠‡∏£‡πå‡∏°‡∏¥‡πÅ‡∏ô‡∏ô‡∏ï‡πå

The determinant is a special scalar value calculated from a square matrix. It reveals how the matrix transforms space. For a 2x2 matrix, its absolute value is the area of the parallelogram formed by its column vectors.
