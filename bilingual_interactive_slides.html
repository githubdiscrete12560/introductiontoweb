What is Location Scanning?

Real-World Digital Mapping Technology

  • Capture detailed 3D representations of physical spaces
  • Create precise digital maps of real-world locations
  • Enable augmented reality (AR) and mixed reality experiences
  • Build spatial databases for navigation and positioning
Key Technology: LiDAR, photogrammetry, SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping)

How Location Scanning Works

The Scanning Process:

  • 1Data Capture: Camera and sensors record visual and spatial data
  • 2Point Cloud Generation: Creates millions of 3D coordinate points
  • 3Mesh Creation: Connects points to form 3D surfaces
  • 4Texture Mapping: Applies visual details and colors
  • 5Optimization: Compresses data for real-time use
  • 6Cloud Processing: Uploads and processes data on remote servers
Result: Accurate digital twin of physical location

Immersal Mapper 2.0 - Introduction

Getting Started with Manual Mode

Immersal Mapper 2.0 is a pre-built application that transforms any physical space into detailed spatial maps or point cloud meshes.

Interface Overview:

  • Top Right: Profile details, user level, and mode selector (M for Manual)
  • Top Left: Refresh button to update map list view
  • Bottom Center: Yellow capture button for creating new maps
  • Three Main Tabs: All Maps, In Cloud, On Device

Two Operating Modes:

  • Manual Mode: Individual image capture with higher quality results
  • Real-Time Mode: Automatic capture but lower quality output

Setting Up Manual Mode

Pre-Capture Setup:

Account and Login:

  • Download Immersal Mapper from App Store or Google Play
  • Leave server setting as 'International Server' (default)
  • Login with your developer credentials

Creating a New Map:

  • Click the plus (+) icon at bottom center to create a new map
  • Ensure Manual Mode is selected by clicking 'M' in the top right corner

Device Preparation:

  • Ensure stable internet connection for uploads
  • Clear sufficient storage space (2-5GB recommended)
  • Consider using devices with LiDAR sensors for enhanced accuracy

Manual Mode Capture Process

Step-by-Step Capture Workflow:

Image Capture Strategy:

  • Point camera at target area and click CAPTURE button while moving horizontally
  • Aim for 50% overlap between consecutive images
  • Cover the environment from multiple viewpoints and distances

Movement Pattern:

  • Walk systematically around your target area
  • Use 'outside-in' method for rooms - move around perimeter looking across space
  • Take images from different positions and angles for optimal results

Visual Feedback:

  • App displays 'Image connected to previous' notification when images successfully match
  • Each image needs visual connection to at least two others for map construction

Security Practices - Data Protection

Essential Security Measures:

Before Scanning:

  • Review app permissions carefully
  • Understand data collection and storage policies
  • Verify the app developer's reputation
  • Check if data is processed locally or in cloud

During Scanning:

  • Avoid scanning sensitive or confidential areas
  • Be aware of personal information visible in scans
  • Consider who has access to your scanning location
  • Remove or cover sensitive documents and screens

Security Practices - Privacy Protection

Protecting Personal and Organizational Privacy:

Location Privacy:

  • Disable location sharing when not necessary
  • Use generic project names instead of specific addresses
  • Consider scanning during off-hours to avoid capturing people
  • Review metadata attached to scanned content

Access Control:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for accounts
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Regularly review and update sharing permissions
  • Limit access to authorized team members only
